What we provide

Our interior design services are here to help you transform your space
into a reflection of your personal style and functional needs.

Consultation

Our initial consultation is the first step in creating your dream space. During this meeting, we'll discuss your design goals, budget, and preferences. We'll also take measurements of your space and give you an estimate for the design plan and services.

Design Plan

We will create a comprehensive design plan that includes a detailed floor plan, furniture layout, color scheme, and a selection of materials. This plan will serve as the blueprint for your project and will be used to guide all the decisions that follow.

Space Planning

Our team will create a functional and visually pleasing layout for your space. We'll take into account traffic flow, lighting, and focal points to create a space that is both beautiful and functional.

Material Selection

We'll help you select the perfect finishes, fabrics, and materials for your space. From flooring to paint colors, we'll ensure that everything is in line with your design plan and budget

Project Management

We'll oversee every aspect of your project, from ordering materials to coordinating with contractors. We'll make sure that everything runs smoothly and that the end result meets your expectations.

Virtual Design

During pandemics or other restrictions, we offer virtual design service to allow you to get the same quality of service from the comfort of your home. We can use video conferencing and 3D rendering software to help you visualize and plan your space.
